Birthday note

Happy birthday to one of the kindest people I know.

I have said this before and will say it again: you are someone I consider really close to the word sunshine, warm, bright, and easy to trust. Something you do not always have to seek, but you know is present. Always looking out for others even when you yourself are in the dumps. Life hasn’t been fair, but look at you still trying to hold the mantle of justice.

You have the rare habit of helping people without making it dramatic. You always do it without making it seem like a big deal, like it is just naturally built into your system. This is not at all something many people possess.

I wish I knew what sorcery is involved when we study together, but productive time with you is one of the most efficient kinds I have felt, easy and simple. The pacing works, and somehow I end up remembering a lot of it as well.

Even after having a very rough year, which you did not deserve, you kept smiling. All the self-loathing, but still making sure everyone else was smiling too, that is some determination I would honestly call ridiculous.

There are really small moments which are legit anchor points in my memory. You would not even have imagined it while you were doing them, but for me they meant a lot. Stuff as simple as ruffling my hair. That is something really nostalgic kyuki friends back home always did it, but ever since I came to VIJ, stupidity and bad life choices included, I do not think anyone ever genuinely felt my hair and praised me for it. I might have honestly cried that evening. It was so niceee to hear that after so long.

Sooooo umm, once a year you get to have this day for yourself. Keep it the way you like it, and nothing less.

Have fuuuun!!
